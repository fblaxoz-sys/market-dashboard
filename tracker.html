Everything runs off a base of 100 units. A 2% buy spends 2 units of cash, so “2%” is the same size whichever year you trade it. Positions, cash and weights are all replayed from the log, so correcting a trade corrects the portfolio. Cost basis is the weighted average across your buys; realized P&L is booked against it when you sell.
Prices are delayed daily closes from Yahoo Finance. This is a tracking tool, not investment advice, and nothing here is a recommendation to buy or sell anything.
